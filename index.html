Coltivare


About Us

At Coltivare, we believe agriculture is the foundation of progress. Since our founding in 1985, we have been dedicated to providing farmers with the tools, technology, and support they need to cultivate stronger harvests and more sustainable futures.

History

What began as a small family workshop in West Lafayette, Indiana, has grown into a trusted provider of agricultural products and services. From precision machinery and advanced irrigation systems to crop nutrition solutions and digital farm management platforms, our product portfolio is designed to help farmers maximize efficiency while protecting their land for generations to come.

Services

Alongside our products, we provide a range of services

1.Soil analysis
2.Sustainability consulting
3.Equipment maintenance
4.Farm management software for tracking costs, yields, and field mappings.
These services allow us to partner with farmers beyond the field, ensuring they have the knowledge and support to adapt to an ever-changing agricultural landscape.

Impact

Today, Coltivare serves farming communities across North America. While we haven't expanded worldwide yet, our mission remains the same as it was on day one: to cultivate progress by empowering farmers with innovative solutions that bring together tradition, technology, and sustainability.
