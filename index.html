About Us

At Coltivare, we believe agriculture is the foundation of progress. Since our founding in 1985, we have been dedicated to providing farmers with the tools, technology, and support they need to cultivate stronger harvests and more sustainable futures.


History


What began as a small family workshop in West Lafayette, Indiana, has grown into a trusted global provider of agricultural products and services. From precision machinery and advanced irrigation systems to crop nutrition solutions and digital farm management platforms, our product portfolio is designed to help farmers maximize efficiency while protecting their land for generations to come.
